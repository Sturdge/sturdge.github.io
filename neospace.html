Neo Space Back

Project Info

  • Genre - Infinite Runner
  • Year - 2020
  • Engine - Unity

Neo Space is an endless runner created as part of a Master's degree in game development.

The project was created as a way of gauging how players interact with games with micro-transactions for a greater study regarding micro-transactions utilising Unity's analytics framework in conjunction with fake micro-transactions to monitor engagement.

Due to the small scope of the project, the project was completed in under 48 hours of work and allowed for the practice of more visually stimulating work without the use of an artist; conjuring up a retro CRT-style display and vapourwave aesthetics.
