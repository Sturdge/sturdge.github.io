Changi of Plans Back

Project Info

  • Genre - Stealth
  • Year - 2015
  • Engine - Unity

Changi of Plan is a stealth game set in a Japanese war camp. It was developed after Yarr! as part of Bachelor's degree. The player is tasked with escaping the camp while avoiding detection by the guards. The player can accomplish this by sneaking around, avoiding puddles, and using items in the environment to distract the guards.
